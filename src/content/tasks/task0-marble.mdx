## Task Brief

The agent receives a forward impulse control with a bounded scalar parameter. Its objective is to stop the marble in the highlighted target area by choosing the right force.

## Why It Matters

This is the smallest useful AgentArk pattern: multimodal observation, executable action generation, immediate physics feedback, and repeated attempts on a fixed seed.
