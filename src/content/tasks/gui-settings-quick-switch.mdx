## Task Brief

Navigate a mock phone Settings app and apply targeted toggles or brightness changes.

## Why It Matters

This task exercises phone-style GUI control where the agent must read visible labels, choose the right app surface, and execute precise taps without disturbing unrelated options.

## Author Note

The task checks visual GUI grounding, tap precision, and whether a model can preserve unrelated settings while changing the requested controls.
