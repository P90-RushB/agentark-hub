## Task Brief

The agent plans a sequence of grid moves to push boxes onto target cells. The challenge is to reason about box constraints before committing actions.

## Why It Matters

Pushbox evaluates spatial planning, irreversible action awareness, and the ability to encode a route as executable environment commands.

## Author Note

Sokoban-style tasks are unforgiving; one locally plausible push can make the entire state unsolvable.
