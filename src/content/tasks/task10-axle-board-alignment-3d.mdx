## Task Brief

Four rectangular boards are mounted along one horizontal axle. The agent issues additional rotations for each board and succeeds when their angle spread is within tolerance.

## Why It Matters

This task measures visual pose estimation, relative alignment, and multi-parameter action composition in a compact 3D scene.
